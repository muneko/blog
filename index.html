What is a 30-day trailing average?

Some States do not report every day or even every week.

To try and take account for this, take 2 dates seperated by 30 days.

Subtract the earlier date from the later date and divide by 30.

Consider it a lagging indicator.

Why does the average for a day change?

If numbers are updated, either up or down,
this changes the average for a day.
